Detailed Description

Analytical term-structure fitting parameter \( \varphi(t) \).

\( \varphi(t) \) is analytically defined by

\[ \varphi(t) = f(t) + \frac{1}{2}[\frac{\sigma(1-e^{-at})}{a}]^2, \]

where \( f(t) \) is the instantaneous forward rate at \( t \).
