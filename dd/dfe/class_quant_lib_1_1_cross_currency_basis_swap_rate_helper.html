Detailed Description

Rate helper for bootstrapping over XCCY basis swap rates.

Constant notional cross currency swap helper. Unlike marked-to-market cross currency swaps, both notionals expressed in base and quote currency remain constant throughout the lifetime of the swap.

For more details see: N. Moreni, A. Pallavicini (2015) FX Modelling in Collateralized Markets: foreign measures, basis curves and pricing formulae.
